Welcome To Online Consulting Service

Prathyasa counselling centre was established in 2007 and is headquartered in Thrissur district, Kerala state in India. Prathyasa counselling centre offers a therapeutic approach that includes a variety of counselling and psychotherapy . The counselling centre has been addressing the psychological and behavioural problems of individual for more than a decade. We emphasis on family counselling, student counselling and marital counselling. Prathyasa also seeks to bring about a change that is holistic in itself using multi-sensory and multidisciplinary approach.
